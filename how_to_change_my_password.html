What to do after my password has been reset?

After clicking on forgot password, your password gets reset and the reset password is sent to your email account. In order to change your password, you need to be connected to the application. Connect with freshly reset password which is sent to your email.

How to change my password?

You can change your password by clicking on the padlock icon at the left of your name (top right corner of every screen of the application):

  • Finding the Padlock icon that changes your password

When you you click on the padlock icon , a modal windows appears and it allows you to reset your password:

  • Window to change your password

Once you've reset your password, you will receive an email with the subject "Your password has been reset". It is meant to inform you of any sensible operation performed on your account. Of course, if you didn't perform this operation, you should immediately report this incident to your HR Admin.

Some suggestions for a good & strong password

A good password is something easy for you to remember, but difficult for others to guess. It is not recommend to use this kind of passwords:

  • A word from the company/workplace
  • A memorable date
  • A simple sequence of numbers or characters (eg "123456" or "qwerty")

It is better to use a combination of letters and characters in lower and upper case. Some punctuation marks or special characters may be used, for example:

1AmTheBest@nEarth!
